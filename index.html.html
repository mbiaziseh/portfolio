About

Bridging Citizens, Institutions, and Evidence

I am a civic innovation leader and policy entrepreneur committed to strengthening democratic governance. My journey began in Cameroon in 2018 with a clear observation: governance systems often operate at a distance from the people they are meant to serve.

Across Africa, fewer than 40% of citizens trust parliaments, and nearly 78% of Africans live in countries where governance conditions have worsened. In this context, citizens — especially young people — remain underrepresented in decision-making, despite making up the majority of the population.

I operate at the intersection of public policy, civic technology, and governance innovation, translating complex systems into practical tools and platforms that people can understand and influence. What defines my approach is the ability to connect research with action and systems with people.

Mission
"To strengthen democratic governance by advancing citizen participation, electoral integrity, and evidence-based policymaking, while building systems that promote transparency, accountability, and informed public engagement."
